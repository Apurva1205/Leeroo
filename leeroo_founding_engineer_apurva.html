How I map to the Founding Engineer role

Customer-first, end-to-end problem solving
  • Comfortable taking ambiguous business asks ("help my team work faster", "clean up this process") and decomposing them into scoped, shippable agent flows: data understanding, context design, evaluation plan, and rollout.
  • Used to working across multiple stakeholders (founders, engineers, non-technical teams) and keeping a tight feedback loop on what is working and what needs re-design.
  • I treat every deployment as a learning loop: instrumenting agents, collecting feedback, and folding patterns back into reusable components.
Building GenAI & agentic applications
  • Practical experience with LLM applications and NLP workflows: text classification, retrieval, ranking, and context engineering.
  • Comfortable with vector databases and retrieval workflows (RAG-style pipelines) to ground agents in company knowledge bases.
  • I think in terms of tools, memories, and policies – how an agent should observe, decide, act, and learn from each episode.
Working with core research & product teams
  • I naturally document reusable patterns from project work: prompts, evaluators, routing logic, data schemas – perfect for feeding into a platform / Learning Engine.
  • Comfortable collaborating with research teams, translating customer pain points into concrete product asks (new tools, memory modules, evaluators, dashboards).
Communication
  • Used to explaining complex systems in simple language – what the agent does, where it can fail, how we’ll monitor it, and what success looks like in business terms.
